What type of makeup should I wear?

To determine your makeup, first identify your skin tone and undertone to choose products like foundation that match your natural complexion. Next, consider your desired look, whether that's enhancing your features or a specific style, and then select products like eyeshadow and blush based on your eye color and skin tone. Finally, don't forget to consider the occasion, your personal style, and to practice blending to perfect your look.
