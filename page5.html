What style is trending right now?

Current trends include oversized silhouettes like baggy jeans and large blazers, a resurgence of Y2K style (early 2000s) with metallic fabrics and low-rise bottoms, and athletic-inspired wear like retro tracksuits. Additionally, preppy and academic styles with a modern twist are popular, along with the "mariner" or sailor-core trend featuring Breton stripes and nautical themes
