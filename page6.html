What nail designs are fashionable?

2025 nail color trends include Mocha Mousse (the Pantone color of the year), rich deep reds and browns, and earthy olive and moss greens for fall. Other popular colors are Tiffany Blue, various shades of pink, and vibrant, almost-primary colors like cobalt blue and lime green for a more maximalist summer look. For a softer, more elegant vibe, trends also include milky pinks and icy, translucent pastels like lilac and baby blue.
